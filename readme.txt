== Description ==

Social Bookmark by DYC is a free way to boost traffic back to your site by making it easier for visitors to share your content on social media sites such as Buzzup, Delicious and Digg.

Our plugin will add buttons on your site pages, that let your visitors easy to share about your site and content on social media sites i.e., on facebook, google plus, twitter, buzz up, digg, delicious, reddit, stumbleUpon and many more.

The services can be added manually into site code by developer or simply using widgets. This will make easier for your visitor to share about your site.

== Installation ==

1. Search for the plugin under WordPress.org Plugin gallery
2. Downlaod the plugin
3. Upload the plugin folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/ directory of your site
4. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu under WordPress Dashboard
5. Keep enjoying the plugin (Lets Rock the World)
